About Us

Our Background

Seventy7Soccer Ltd., a Calabar-based sports development company, aspires to revolutionize sports standards and cultivate high-quality talent in Nigeria. We provide a world-class curriculum that equips young athletes with the technical and tactical skills needed to compete internationally. Our founder, Fidel Egbe, a passionate coach and mentor, returns to his home state, Cross River, to share his expertise and build the next generation of sports stars.

Mission
To inspire and nurture soccer talent through comprehensive training, unwavering support, and community engagement. We aim to empower players of all ages and skill levels by fostering positivity.
Vision
Our vision is to become a leading platform for soccer development, renowned for cultivating world-class talent and making a lasting impact in the global soccer community.

Why Us

Why Choose Seventy7Soccer?

Seventy7Soccer offers a unique platform dedicated to fostering soccer talent through comprehensive training, community engagement, tournaments and growth opportunities. With a personalized Focus, we work with players at all skill levels to ensure they reach their full potential both on and off the field.

Our founder, Mr Egbe's experience as a UEFA licensed soccer coach, currently leading the Orchard Valley Toros FC. His dedication to developing young talent is evident in his coaching career. As part of our initial contribution, we successfully launched an essay competition on sports and mental health in July 2020, receiving over a hundred entries and recruiting two talented individuals who now work with us.
